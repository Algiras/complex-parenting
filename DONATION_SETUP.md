**Why**: 0% fees on one-time, 5% on subscriptions, cleaner UI

3. Add ‚Ç¨5,000 audiobook goal

### 4. **PayPal** (FAMILIAR TO EVERYONE)
**Why**: Most people already have it, ~3% fees

3. No goal tracking (just a payment button)

## ü™ô Cryptocurrency Setup (Optional)

### Why Crypto?
- 0% fees (except network gas)
- Privacy for donors
- International (no PayPal restrictions)

### What You Need:
1. **Bitcoin Wallet**: Electrum (free, secure)
2. **Ethereum Wallet**: MetaMask (browser extension)
3. **Monero Wallet**: MyMonero (privacy-focused)

### Setup:
1. Download wallets
2. Generate receive addresses
3. Add to `24-support.qmd`

**Warning**: You're responsible for tax reporting on crypto donations.
